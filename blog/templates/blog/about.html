About Me!

My name is Debbie Thompson and I have been a maths teacher for 28 years

25 of those years were spent working in secondary schools.

I now work as an online tutor of maths and computer science and have a keen

interest in working on full stack apps using the django and python frameworks!


In my time working as an online tutor and full time teacher I have developed many

skills and a resources bank. It will be great to share these ideas with other tutors.

So the idea behind creating this app is for all tutors to be able to share ideas on teaching

maths and computer science so that all tutors can have access to these ideas and resources.


Please take a look at the blog and resources pages to see if there is anything there

that may be of interest to you and also please do add any ideas or resources that you

have that you think other teachers might find useful.
